Message from the President

Greetings from the President’s Desk,

As we stand to begin the new academic session, I the President of SUFIYA HAMEEDIA TRUST and the principal of SUFIYA HAMEEDIA Group of Institutions extend a hearty and warm welcome to all my students, staff and parents. Each academic year is a new height scaled; new targets set for innovation in the future at all institutions. The preaching, teaching and the learning legacy of “ASHAB-E-SUFFA” and the ‘SUFIYA-E-KARAM’, set as example by one of our scholar Qutub-E-Banaras ‘Asshah Abdul Hameed Alai-Hir-Rahmah’ is followed as an identity of Ahle-Sunnnat-Wa-Jamat at Sufiya Hameedia Group of Institutions.

Our dedicated and caring staffs play exemplary role models for our students. Each member of our institution is devoted to turning dreams & aspirations into reality through sincerity & perseverance.

We wish to thank all the parents for their faith in SUFIYA HAMEEDIA Group of Institutions. Let’s partner and move together to see that the children are successful in whatever path they choose to tread.

Academic Institutions

At Sufiya Hameedia we always tried to reach out the needy learners of all the age groups by fostering their individual creativity, social skills, Islamic (Deeni) and academic intellect for all round growth and development of personality. We follow beyond the conventional model with innovative and experiential practices of learning from our 20 years of educational legacy of SUFIYA HAMEEDIA GROUPS OF INSTITUTIONS.

To achieve the goal of the new education policy, NEP-2020, the SUFIYA HAMEEDIA TRUST® runs three academic programs to educate diverse groups of learners.

Higher Primary School

Sufiya Hameedia HPS (Higher Primary School) covers LKG, UKG and Grades 1 - 5. It is a complete English Medium Day School recognized by Department of School Education and Literacy, Government of Karnataka with Grades 6th to 10th starting soon upon approval from the Government.

Learn More

Arabic Madrasa

Sufiya Hameedia Arabic Madrasa follows Hafiz-e-Quran, Nazrah Quran with Tajweed and other Islamic practices by catering the need of those students who find free hours in the morning and evening. We are planning to follow a comprehensive curriculum with TAHTANIYA, MOLVI, AALIM and FAZILAT in near future.

Learn More

Adult Education Institution (Night Coaching)

Sufiya Hameedia Adult Education Institution caters to adult learners who were not able complete their formal education but have the zeal to graduate. The Adult Education Institution also teaches uneducated adults and adults with very low literacy to make them ready for the digital world. As these groups of candidates are busy providing for their families during the day, separate coaching class are held at night between 9:00pm and 11:00pm for individual learners of any age group.
